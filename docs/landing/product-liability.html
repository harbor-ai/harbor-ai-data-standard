We Partner With Agents To Place Product Liability

The Harbor.ai platform was built to help agents. Our AI-powered E&S insurance exchange provides retail brokers with a wholesale market access point. When a submission is received, it’s funneled through our product-appetite matrix, which intelligently finds the best corresponding coverage opportunities based on carrier appetites, industry, and lines of coverage. Through our carrier partners, we have direct access to admitted and non-admitted markets, and MGAs. As an added plus to agents, we streamline the nitty-gritty with pre-filled supplemental applications, automated follow-ups and servicing, and a compliance tool.

Among our product lines is Product Liability insurance. The Harbor.ai team works to place customizable coverage and protect individuals and organizations from their businesses’ unique risks. Premium indications can be provided in minutes!
