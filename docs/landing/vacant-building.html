Insurance for Vacant Buildings

When you own a vacant building, whether residential or commercial, you face the issue of having an unsupervised property. At Harbor.ai, we understand that the unique risks associated with unoccupied properties are part and parcel of being a building owner, and we want to help you protect your property.

Our insurance agents can connect you to insurance companies who can provide comprehensive coverage while lowering your insurance costs. Contact us today for a free quote.

Vacant properties constantly face claims. When a third party sustains an injury on your unoccupied property, you don’t want to be stuck with the legal and medical costs of a bodily injury claim. Whether an accident stems from a sidewalk crack or siding, our insurance carrier partners have you covered. Don’t let a third party injury claim set back your property business with financial losses. We can help protect you against:

  • Bodily Injury Claims: A third-party files a claim for injury on vacant property premises.
  • Personal Injury / Advertising Injury Claims: A third-party claims that their image or reputation was damaged as a result of your electrical installation.
  • Property Damage Claims: A third-party claims that their property was damaged as a result of an incident, such as a gas leak, that occurred at your vacant property.

Vacant property insurance is generally added in addition to standard residential or commercial property policies. Coverage can include infrastructure damage, weather damage, theft, and vandalism. Equipment breakdown insurance is also available for damages caused by internal sources, for instance power surges, mechanical breakdowns, or motor burnouts. If a boiler malfunctions and causes a leak, an equipment breakdown policy may help cover related losses.

At Harbor.ai, we connect building owners with our insurance partners best able to cover your property, occupied or not.

Flood insurance is available for both residential and commercial properties and Harbor.ai can help you find the right coverage. It’s not generally included in property insurance policies, but is an essential protection for building owners. Flood insurance policies can help deal with water damage to furniture, equipment, floors, walls, and ceilings due to flooding. Our agents can work with you to get the best flood policy for your property.

Already have an existing general liability or auto liability insurance policy? We can connect you with an insurance company willing to increase your limit of liability and expand your existing coverage.
