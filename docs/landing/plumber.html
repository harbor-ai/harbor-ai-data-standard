Plumbers Insurance — Protect Your Plumbing Business

At Harbor.ai, we understand the unique risks that plumbing contractors confront. Plumbing contractors are often sole proprietors or small businesses, and the financial losses from unanticipated claims can set back businesses for years.

We partner with insurance companies who provide comprehensive coverage while lowering your insurance cost. Contact our insurance agents to get your free quote today.

We understand that accidents happen. As a plumber, you may confront claims from customers that far outweigh the often small mistakes that cause them. Slips and falls, excessive noise, and property damage are all potential risks to customers. In off-premises work, injury and damage to third-parties are real risks. We can help protect you against:

  • Bodily Injury Claims: A client or other third-party is injured due to or during a plumbing operation.
  • Personal Injury / Advertising Injury Claims: A third-party claims that their image or reputation was damaged as a result of your electrical installation.
  • Property Damage Claims: A customer claims that their property was damaged during the course of or due to a plumbing operation.
  • Additional GL Coverages: To help meet your contract needs, we ensure that your policy includes the following enhancements on a blanket basis, as necessary.
    • Additional Insureds
    • Primary and Noncontributory Endorsements
    • Waiver of Subrogation

Get property insurance coverage for the physical location of your business, leased or owned, and the equipment you use on- and off-site daily. At Harbor.ai, we connect plumbing contractors with our insurance partners to cover the risks of:

  • Buildings and Contents: Cover your workplace, storage spaces, and the tangible property inside of them in the event of damage or destruction.
  • Equipment Breakdown: Equipment Breakdown: If a pipe cutter breaks down or a machine needs to be replaced, equipment breakdown insurance helps cover the costs of repair and replacement.

Whether headed to a residence, a commercial building, or a factory, plumbing contractors travel to off-site locations every day. Commercial auto insurance protects business owners against the risks of damage to company vehicles, whether replacements and breakdowns, accidents and damage, or associated medical costs. We can help you find the best coverage option for your vehicles from our insurance carrier partners.

By law, business owners are required to protect their employees with workers compensation and employers liability insurance. Plumbers are always busy and their work requires a unique combination of skill, physical labor, and experience. Plumbing contractors work with sealants, chemicals, and power equipment, and also typically work in confined spaces. Our insurance agents can help you to find a workers compensation and employers liability policy that covers the high risk of employee injury and lowers your insurance costs.

Things don’t always go according to plan. Small errors in design or installation can result in costly leaks. Incorrect fittings can cause unanticipated water damage. A hiccup in waste and vent pipe maintenance can cause fires. Product liability and completed operations insurance can protect your business from these claims and the financial losses that follow them.

Plumbing contractors transport their tools, materials, and equipment to job sites every day and need protection from risks such as damage and theft. Contractors can get a Contractors’ Equipment inland marine policy to cover power drills, pipe cutters, pipe-bending machinery, and other equipment. Our agents can also connect you with insurance companies who provide mobile equipment inland marine coverage, protecting equipment in-transit and on the job. At Harbor.ai, we can help you determine the optimal type of inland marine protection for your business.

Already have an existing general liability or auto liability insurance policy? We can connect you with an insurance company willing to increase your limit of liability and expand your existing coverage.
